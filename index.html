Executive Overview

A platform that leverages generative AI to convert long-form writing and audio content into meme-worthy, highly shareable snippets, helping content creators quickly engage broader audiences on social media.

Problem Statement

Content creators with substantial followings often struggle to attract audience attention for long-form content that requires significant time commitment, leading to lower engagement and underutilization of valuable insights.

Solution Statement

The platform utilizes generative AI to distill lengthy text and audio into engaging, meme-worthy content that captures key messages and is easy to share on social media, increasing content accessibility and audience engagement.

The target market includes the vast and growing community of content creators, influencers, educators, and thought leaders who are shifting towards short-form, highly engaging content.
